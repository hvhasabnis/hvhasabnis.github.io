Teaching Python Best Practices to Middle School Robotics Students: Curriculum Design and Instructor Reflections

Abbas Attarwala, Paul R. Viotti, Mohammed Albahttiti, Hrishikesh Vasant Hasabnis

Journal of Computing Sciences in Colleges (CCSC), Volume 41, Number 6, pp. 142-157

32nd Annual CCSC Central Plains Conference, Drury University, Springfield, MO

PDF
This paper presents a comprehensive curriculum for teaching Python programming best practices to middle school robotics students. We describe our pedagogical approach, curriculum design principles, and reflections from implementing the program. The research provides insights into effective strategies for introducing programming concepts to young learners in a robotics context.
2026

Sonifying Kepler's Harmonice Mundi: A Csound Implementation for Teaching Scientific Sonification

Paul R. Viotti, Abbas Attarwala, Hrishikesh Vasant Hasabnis

Journal of Computing Sciences in Colleges (CCSC), Volume 41, Number 6, pp. 166-176

32nd Annual CCSC Central Plains Conference, Drury University, Springfield, MO

PDF
We present a Csound-based implementation for sonifying Kepler's Harmonice Mundi, translating astronomical data into auditory representations. This work demonstrates the educational value of scientific sonification as a tool for teaching astronomy and physics concepts through sound synthesis and data-to-audio mapping techniques.
